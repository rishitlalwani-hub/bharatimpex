About Us

Five Decades of Manufacturing Excellence

Established in 1972, Bharat Impex has earned a significant place in the overseas market as a prominent manufacturer and exporter of brass sanitary ware, pipe fittings, taps, bathroom fittings, and accessories. Our state-of-the-art production units, imported raw materials, and team of expert professionals deliver world-class products as per British Standard Specifications.
